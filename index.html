01The Driver
The Driver

Talent.
Intelligence.
Hard work.

Born in Monte Carlo on 16 October 1997, Charles Leclerc first climbed into a kart at three and a half — and never really climbed back out.

He worked through the karting ranks, made his F1 debut with Sauber in 2018, and joined Scuderia Ferrari in 2019.
