02 The Beginnings
Chapter 02 — Beginnings

A passion
that became
a dream.

A kart, a Monaco hillside, and a kid who knew at three years old.

Charles ran his first kart on the track managed by Jules Bianchi's father. A decade later it was Jules — by then a close friend — who urged Nicolas Todt to come watch the next race.

Kart Results

Five seasons, five records.

03 Motorsport
Chapter 03 — Motorsport

From karts to
single-seaters.

When Nicolas Todt joined his team, the shape of Charles' career changed. The step up to single-seaters in 2014 came with the usual hurdles — and he finished his rookie season in second place.

04 World of Ferrari
Chapter 04 — Ferrari

A dream
in red.

When he played with toy cars, he always picked the red one.

In 2019, the year he turned 22, Charles joined Scuderia Ferrari — the second-youngest driver in the team's history. Wins followed at Monza (2019, 2024) and at home in Monte Carlo (2024).
